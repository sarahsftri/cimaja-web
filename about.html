About CIMAJA Eco Retreat

CIMAJA Retreat is situated on the southwest coast of Cimaja and spans 250 hectares of oceanfront and foreshore adventures. We’re six beautiful bungalow, an oceanfront restaurant, lounge, yoga studio/ spa space, beaches, and a magnificent pool. We cater to the discerning traveler eager to get off the beaten path and into an adventure with just enough raw luxury. A family run and community minded affair with fifteen years of experience on island we’ve really been able to pour years of great Cimaja experience into the design and implementation of this divine retreat. We’ve built CIMAJA with the three tenants of Permaculture in mind. Earth Care, People Care, and Fair Share.
